What is Mangamop?

Mangamop is an automated manga cleaning web application that uses a deep learning Pix2Pix model to translate images. (Github).

Manga cleaning is often a difficult process that requires many steps and a lot of human oversight. But, with deep learning, we can simplify the process to a simple click of a button! (See Examples)

Upload your raw manga scans to see the magic happen!

  • Get Started

    • ** This model still may not be able to clean manga as well as human cleaners. We simply hope running your raw scans through this model could help you start off with a better base scan and allow you to skip some of the inital steps!

How it Works

Just drag-and-drop or upload your raw manga scans, wait for the model to process your images, and download the results. It's that easy!

Step 1 - Upload

Upload multiple image files at once! The model was trained on raw print scans, which usually require a lot more work to clean as opposed to raw digital scans. For the best results, it is recommended that your manga raws are similar to some of the scans in the Examples section. Of course, the model is still a work in progress, and hopefully in the future, it will become flexible enough to cover all types of raws.
